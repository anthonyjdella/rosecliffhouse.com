About

Experience the epitome of Pacific Northwest living at the Rose Cliff House, where breathtaking views of Mt Rainier and Puget Sound await at every turn. This spacious mid-century home has been thoughtfully updated for modern comfort and seamless entertaining.

Step through imported antique gates into the private front courtyard, setting the stage for the elegance within. The main floor boasts a luxurious primary bedroom suite, a versatile office/den, and a second full bath, along with convenient washer/dryer access. The living room features a cozy fireplace, while the expansive view great/family room showcases vaulted ceilings and acacia wood flooring. Italian marble accents the modern chef's kitchen, complemented by a huge dining room with French doors opening to an amazing view deck.

Natural light pours through expansive south-facing windows, illuminating the living and dining areas while framing stunning vistas. Downstairs, discover a second kitchen, three additional bedrooms, a second family room, and one and a half baths, along with a utility room boasting ample storage.

Outdoors, enjoy a Saltwater Swim Spa, a charming playhouse, and an inviting outdoor gas fireplace, perfect for year-round enjoyment. A convenient carport and serene location on a quiet street complete this unparalleled offering in one of the region's most desirable neighborhoods.

Discover the Rose Cliff House, where panoramic views and luxurious living converge in perfect harmony.
